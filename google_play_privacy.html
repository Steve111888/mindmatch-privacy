Your Privacy Matters: MindMatch is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Name, age, gender, date of birth, email address, phone number
  • Photos: Profile photos and images you upload (up to 6 photos)
  • Personality Data: MBTI type, interests, hobbies, bio, preferences
  • Communication: Messages, chat conversations with matches
  • Location: Approximate geographic location (city/region) for local matching
  • Account Credentials: Username, encrypted password, authentication tokens

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App interactions, swipe patterns, time spent in app
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Preferences: Match preferences, filter settings, notification settings

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Matchmaking: Using AI algorithms to find compatible matches based on personality, interests, and preferences
  • Profile Display: Showing your profile to potential matches
  • Communication: Enabling chat and messaging with your matches
  • Location-Based Matching: Finding matches in your geographic area

2.2 Improvement and Analytics

  • Improving matching algorithms and app performance
  • Analyzing user behavior to enhance user experience
  • Developing new features and functionality
  • Conducting research and testing

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Verifying user identity and preventing fraud
  • Detecting and preventing abuse, harassment, or violations
  • Protecting against security threats
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service

2.4 Communication

  • Sending match notifications and app updates
  • Responding to customer support inquiries
  • Sending promotional messages (with your consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

Your profile information (name, photos, bio, age, interests) is visible to other MindMatch users for matching purposes.

3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate MindMatch:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud storage, push notifications
  • AI Services: Personality analysis and matching algorithms
  • Cloud Hosting: Data storage and app infrastructure
  • Analytics: App performance and usage analytics (anonymized)

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate fraud or violations
  • Respond to government requests

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

We DO NOT: Sell your personal information to third parties or share it with advertisers without your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed and encrypted using bcrypt
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in Firebase with security rules and access controls
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing

4.2 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
  • Backups: Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

4.3 International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

  • Access: View and edit your profile information anytime in app settings
  • Update: Change your photos, bio, preferences at any time
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account from settings

5.2 Privacy Controls

  • Location: Control location sharing in device settings
  • Notifications: Manage push notification preferences
  • Visibility: Control who can see your profile
  • Blocking: Block specific users from contacting you

5.3 Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@mindmatch.app
